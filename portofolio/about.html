About

Jennifer Fernandez is a Brooklyn-based writer and editor. She has been on staff at Architectural Digest, Travel + Leisure, and Martha Stewart Weddings; her work has also appeared in The Wall Street Journal, Elle Decor, House Beautiful, Afar, Departures, and more. On the brand side, she has worked with Sotheby's International Realty, Home Depot, Zappos, Brooklinen, and others. Contact her at jenfernandezbk@gmail.com.
